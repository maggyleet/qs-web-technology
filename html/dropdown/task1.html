Apple

Apples are sweet, red fruits rich in fiber and antioxidants. They help with digestion, improve heart health, and are great as a snack or in pies, juices, and salads.

Banana

Bananas are soft, yellow fruits that are high in potassium. They're great for quick energy, muscle function, and digestion. Popular in smoothies, cereals, and desserts.

Custard Apple

Custard apples, also known as sugar apples or sitaphal, have a sweet, creamy white pulp inside a bumpy green skin. They're rich in vitamins, antioxidants, and are often enjoyed fresh or in milkshakes and desserts.
